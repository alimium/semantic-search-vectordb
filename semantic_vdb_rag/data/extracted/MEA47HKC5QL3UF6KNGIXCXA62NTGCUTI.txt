387.220   Repealed, effective July 1, 1982. 
Catchline at repeal:  Inquest required for appointment of committee -- Oath to jury -- 
Functions of "visitor." 
History: Repealed 1982 Ky. Acts ch. 141, sec. 146, effective July 1, 1982. -- Amended 
1976 (1st Extra. Sess.) Ky. Acts ch. 14, sec. 339, effective January 2, 1978. -- 
Amended 1976 Ky. Acts ch. 218, sec. 40. -- Recodified 1942 Ky. Acts ch. 208, 
sec. 1, effective October 1, 1942, from Ky. Stat. secs. 2151, 2155. 
Note  1980 Ky. Acts ch. 396, sec. 150 would have repealed this section effective July 1, 
1982. However, 1980 Ky. Acts ch. 396 was repealed by 1982 Ky. Acts ch. 141, 
sec. 146, also effective July 1, 1982.
