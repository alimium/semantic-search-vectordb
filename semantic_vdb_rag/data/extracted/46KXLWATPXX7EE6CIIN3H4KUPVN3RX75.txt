Subject:         Budget Act of 2008 Control Language – CalPERS Budget and Expenditure 
Report through First Quarter of Fiscal Year (FY) 2008-09. 
 
In accordance with control language included in the Budget Act of 2008,  
Item 1900-015-0830 (1)(c) , California Public Employees’ Retirement System 
(CalPERS) respectfully submits its FY 2008-09 first quarter report as approved 
by the CalPERS’ Board of Administration at the December 17, 2008, meeting. 
 
The enclosed report includes the following items for FY 2008-09: 
 
• Budget and Expenditure Summary by Function and Division 
• Summary Charts by Function 
• Graphical Display of Budget and Expenditure by Function 
• Position Summary 
• Projected and Actual Investment Expenditures 
• Directed Brokerage Commissions and Expenditures 
• CalPERS Headquarters Building Account Budget/Income/Expenditure 
Summary 
• Expenditures by Fund Source Summary
