§ 28.95
Change of packages for expor-
tation. 
Whenever the exporter desires to 
transfer distilled spirits from packages 
filled in internal revenue bond to such 
other suitable packages as may be de-
sired for exportation, such change of 
packages shall be made under the pro-

to the preparation of TTB Form 5100.11 
covering the removal of the distilled 
spirits.

§ 28.93
Carrier to be designated. 
The name of the carrier or carriers to 
be used in transporting the distilled 
spirits from the bonded premises of the 
distilled spirits plant to the port of ex-
port, or to the customs bonded ware-
house, or to the manufacturing bonded 
warehouse, or to the foreign-trade 
zone, as the case may be, shall be 
shown in the application. If the spirits 
are shipped on a through bill of lading 
and all carriers handling the spirits 
while in transit are not known, the 
name of the carrier to whom the dis-
tilled spirits are to be delivered at the 
shipping premises shall be shown. 
§ 28.96
Approval of application. 
When filed as an application, and 
TTB Form 5100.11 has been properly ex-
ecuted, and the required bond has been 
filed in a sufficient amount, the appro-
priate TTB officer shall approve the 
application on all copies of the form 
and send them to the proprietor of the 
bonded premises from which the spirits 
will be withdrawn.
